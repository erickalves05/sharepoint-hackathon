# My Work Hub — Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 4–5 minutes (well under 8 min limit)  
**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover only

---

## Intro (30 sec)

> "Hi. This is My Work Hub — a SharePoint web part that brings your tasks, meetings, approvals, and recent files into one place, with Copilot to summarize your day."

**[Show web part on a SharePoint page. Point to the title and tabs.]**

---

## Tasks Tab (1 min)

> "The Tasks tab combines Microsoft To Do and Planner in one list."

**[Scroll through tasks.]**

> "Each task shows the source — To Do, Planner, or a flagged email from Outlook. You see the list or plan name, and due dates. Today and tomorrow are highlighted."

**[Point to a Today badge, then a Tomorrow badge.]**

> "To complete a task, click the checkbox. The task gets a strikethrough and stays in the list until you refresh. You can open any task in its app by clicking the icon."

**[Click a checkbox, then click an icon to open.]**

---

## Meetings Tab (30 sec)

> "The Meetings tab shows your next five upcoming events. Same date badges — today, tomorrow — plus the organizer and time. Click to open the meeting."

**[Scroll through meetings, click one.]**

---

## Approvals Tab (45 sec)

> "Approvals shows pending requests from Teams or Power Automate. Descriptions are rendered as markdown. You see who requested it and when."

**[Show an approval with description and owner.]**

> "To reject, you click Reject. A dialog asks for a reason — optional but helpful for the requester."

**[Click Reject, show dialog, optionally type a reason, submit.]**

---

## Recent Tab (30 sec)

> "Recent shows your latest OneDrive files. Icons by type — Excel, Word, PowerPoint, PDF. Click to open. You see the creator and last modified date."

**[Scroll, click a file.]**

---

## Summary Tab (1 min)

> "The Summary tab uses Microsoft 365 Copilot. Click the button to summarize your pending work."

**[Click Summarize.]**

> "We send Copilot your tasks, approvals, and recent files. It prioritizes that data and suggests concrete next steps. The response is formatted as markdown."

**[Show the summary loading, then the result.]**

---

## Wrap-up (30 sec)

> "That’s My Work Hub — one place for tasks, meetings, approvals, and recent files, with Copilot to help you focus. Thanks for watching."

**[Optional: show property pane with the configurable title.]**

---

## Tips for recording

- **Pace:** Speak slowly and clearly. Pauses are fine.
- **Retakes:** If you flub a line, pause and re-record that part. Edit later.
- **Screen:** Keep mouse movements smooth. Avoid rapid clicks.
- **Length:** This script is ~4–5 minutes. You can trim or skip sections if needed.
